Odin's Lasagna

Description

To create a mouthwatering lasagna, start by preparing a rich meat sauce with ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomato sauce, seasoned with basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with beaten eggs, parsley, and a touch of nutmeg for extra flavor. Pre-cook lasagna noodles until they are just al dente. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients starting with a thin layer of meat sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, shredded mozzarella, and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan. Repeat these layers, ending with a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan. Cover the dish with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving to allow the layers to set. Enjoy this classic Italian comfort dish with a side salad and garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 450 g beef mince
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 100 ml water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 450 g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 300 g mozzarella cheese
  • 150 g parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the meat before adding the garlic and onions and cooking until tender.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and tomato puree.
  3. Add in the dried herbs before simmering for 30 minutes.
  4. Cook the lasagna sheets accoring to the packet instructions.
  5. Combine the ricotta cheese, egg and chopped parsley.
  6. Layer meat, lasagna sheets (topped with the ricotta mixture) and mozzarella cheese in repeat layers.
  7. Cook in pre-heated oven for 50 minutes at 190°C.
